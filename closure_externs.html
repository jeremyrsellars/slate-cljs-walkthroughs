Closure Advanced Compliation Externs

To generate extern files, see the project README.md

Use slatecljs.externs.core/externs-str to generate externs.

ClojureScript

(defn- externs-string   []   (with-out-str     (print-externs {:js-var-name "Slate"      :ns-name "slate"}       slate)     (print-externs "slate-react.ext.js" {:js-var-name "SlateReact" :ns-name "slate-react"} slate-react)))
